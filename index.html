About Me

✦ the maker behind the magic ✦

Hi, I'm Angie — the hands, heart, and soul behind Willow's Loom. I create handcrafted jewelry from my home studio, pouring intention into every piece I make. From resin moon pendants to wire-wrapped gemstones, each creation starts with a spark of magic and ends with something you can carry with you always.

I've always believed that the things we wear should mean something. That's why I take my time with every single piece — choosing materials that feel right, working with my hands until it's perfect, and sending each order out with love.

✦ The Dream ✦

Every purchase you make is helping me build toward something bigger — a life on the open road. My dream is to live in a van, travel freely, and create jewelry under open skies in every corner of this beautiful country. You're not just buying jewelry. You're part of the journey.
